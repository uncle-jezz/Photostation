Thrill of Paragliding in Kalimpong

November 11th 2021 by Jayatsen Sharma

The beauty of paragliding is that each flight is different, and the changing seasons bring new colors to the landscapes below. During spring, the meadows come alive with vibrant flowers, while the monsoon showers transform the region into a lush green paradise. Autumn brings warm hues, as the foliage undergoes a stunning transformation.
